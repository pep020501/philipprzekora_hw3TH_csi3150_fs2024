Pikes Peak Highway

Being one of the highest summits in the state, a nineteen-mile long drive up to the
Pikes Peak summit is one of the best ways to experience Colroado's breathtaking
scenic mountain views. Explore not only the beautiful scenery but the various rest
stops and giftshops, including the visitor center at the highest point of the summit
where visitors have the option to grab something to eat or buy souveniers.

What To Wear

Whether it's summer or fall, there is always guaranteed to be cold temperatures
and snow going up the summit. Dress in warm clothes as well as comfortable shoes
as you do have the option to walk around the summit if you so please.
