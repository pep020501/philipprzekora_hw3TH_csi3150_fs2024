Pikes Peak Highway

Being one of the highest summits in the state, a nineteen-mile long drive up to the
Pikes Peak summit is one of the best ways to experience Colroado's breathtaking
scenic mountain views. Explore not only the beautiful scenery but the various rest
stops and giftshops, including the visitor center at the highest point of the summit
where visitors have the option to grab something to eat or buy souveniers.

What To Wear

Whether it's summer or fall, there is always guaranteed to be cold temperatures and
snow going up the summit. Dress in warm clothes as well as comfortable shoes as you
do have the option to walk around the summit if you so please. You should also pack
sunglasses and sunscreen since you will be in closer proximity to the sun.

Reservations

If you plan on driving up the summit between May and October with your own vehicle,
a reservation is required to be placed at least two hours before your arrival in addition to
the purchase of a ticket. Additionally, shuttle options are also avaliable if you would rather
take one up the mountain instead of doing the drive yourself. More information on these rates
and prices can be found here or by calling us at directly at 1-800-###-####.
